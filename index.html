About Us

Founded in the year 1988, Translindo Group core business was merely as customs clearance agency when it first started

Troughout the years, Translindo Group expanded its service offering a suite of solutions that include Shipping Agencies, International Freight Forwarding, Custom Brokerage, Logistics Services, Trucking and Warehousing

Driven by a group of outstanding experts with decades of experiences, today, Translindo Group has grown from a single port in Jakarta to spread widely across main ports in Indonesia such as Surabaya, Semarang, Medan, Bali, and Bandung. The size of our employees has also increase tremendously to approximately 160 people.

We pride ourselves with a good reputation and wide network services. With our trained and dedicated staffs, we work closely with customers to ensure that their needs are met and cargo activities are handled with care. We aim to bring the most reliable shipping services to satisfy our customers.
